Webcam Poser Classifier

Instructions: Make a pose and hold it while pressing the 'Pose A' button a few times until the prediction for A is 1. Make a second pose and hold it while pressing the 'Pose B' button a few times until the prediction for B is 1. Make a third pose and hold it while pressing the 'Pose C' button a few times until the prediction for C is 1.

Suggested poses: Peace sign, Thumbs up, Jazz Hands

Once the model is trained you can then switch between poses and the model should show you your pose predicition in the top right correctly.

This simple application is built using Tensorflow.js and follows the tutorial on their site here: https://codelabs.developers.google.com/codelabs/tensorflowjs-teachablemachine-codelab/index.html
